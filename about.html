Our Story

Serving fries with a passion since 2014, Saucy Frites is a Montreal institution. When Maude Tremblay started her restaurant she knew she had a calling to line the bellies of the rowdy drunks pouring out of the many bars on St-Laurent at closing time, but she also knew she wanted to make it fancy. That's why Saucy Frites uses nothing but the finest ingredients slathered in their trademark sauce on a bed of golden crispy fries. C'est bon!


Located in the heart of Montreal's trendy Plateau neighbourhood, Saucy Frites invites you to discover the gourmet possibilities of Quebec's traditional dish. Thank you for supporting local businesses.
